Random Deathmath & Vehicle Deathmatch

Before a player can kill another player including the police & EMS, all players must be engaged in the active roleplaying scenario. You are not allowed to kill an individual without any roleplay, this also includes the use of vehicles.

 

Realistic Behavior

All players shall conduct themselves in a realistic manner. Despite game mechanics, it is the player's responsibility to act "realistic." Make sure to use your best judgement when it comes to sticking to your role. Understand the boundaries that your character may have and try to stick within those boundaries!

 

Meta-Gaming is Forbidden

You are not allowed to use a players ID to gain knowledge of a players location inside a building, inside a vehicle with tinted windows or recognizing a player by their ID. The use of ''Out of character'' chat is not allowed when talking to other players In-character.

 

FailRP

This is when a player, fails to roleplay in a suitable matter that would cause them to gain an advantage over another player. For example, pulling a shotgun out of no realistic places or talking whilst dead. This also includes failure to use a microphone.

 

Powergaming is Forbidden

Do not abuse in-game mechanics to give you unrealistic advantages towards other players. All kinds of cheating are prohibited.

 

Cop Baiting

It is forbidden for players to bait officers. If an officer is busy with a crime scene such as a bank robbery or a murder or they are engaged in role play, be sure to let them finish. Do not Intentionally hit, ram, shoot, or otherwise act inappropriately toward police personnel without engaging in proper role play.

 

Must Have a Microphone

Players without microphones are subject to being kicked. The "N" key is the default push-to-talk while in game.

 

New Life Rules

If your character dies in a realistic manner (i.e. being burned alive, shot multiple times, stabbed, or even involved in a serious vehicle accident), you are not to "remember" anything from your previous life.EMS or Police are to determine your death.

 

Pursuit Rules

If your vehicle hits a wall, tree, light pole, or any other object over 40 MPH. Then it is now disabled and you can no longer flee using the vehicle. However, you may get out of your vehicle and flee on foot. If you are able flee and get away, you may not change vehicles or clothing/skin for 5 minutes. You may steal another vehicle on the street if you are able to flee after 5 minutes. At no time in a pursuit you allowed to take your vehicle to a custom shop for repairs.

 

Traffic Laws

Speed limit around the city is 45 MPH. Speed Limit on the highway/freeway are 70 MPH. Parking Lots are 25 MPH. Please obey all traffic laws, if not you will be fined or jail accordingly.

 

Combat Logging

Do not respawn while involved in an active roleplaying situation. Logging out of the server to avoid incarceration, or an RP situation is also prohibited.

 

Bank Rules

The bank vault is only open if there are three officers online. Using the bank teller is authorized regardless if officers are on or offline.

 

Weapons

Legal weapons and a firearm permit are available at the gun store for purchase. There are black market weapons available for purchase, but use those weapon at your own risk.

 

DO NOT SPAM CHAT

The chat box is a privilege and not a right. Abusing it will get you kicked or banned. No matter what the issue is, no player is authorized to spam chat. You will be warned, kicked, then banned. You must use /OOC for any OOC discussions. Use /report in chat when notifying an admin of a player abusing the rules, game mechanics, etc.
