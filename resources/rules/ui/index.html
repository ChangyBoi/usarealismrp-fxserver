Random Deathmath & Vehicle Deathmatch

It is forbidden for other players to kill each other without little to no roleplay. This is to include the use of vehicles. Before a player can kill another player, to include the police & EMS, all players must be engaged in roleplay. You are not allowed to kill an individual with role play.

 

Realistic Behavior

All players shall conduct themselves in a realistic manner. Despite game mechanics, it is the player's responsibility to act "realistic." Make sure to use your best judgement when it comes to sticking to your role. Understand the boundaries that your character may have and try to stick within those boundaries! PassiveRP classes (Chef, Doctor, Paramedics, Firemen, Chauffeur, etc) are not going to be committing street crimes, carrying a weapon, or anything that you know they wouldn't do! Be realistic, but make sure it falls within the rules too.

 

Meta-Gaming is Forbidden

All players have their ID numbers's above their heads which is to assist admins and law enforcement (special LEO commands). Players, to include officers & EMS, are not allowed to use a player's ID number to find them behind buildings, inside vehicles with tented windows, or "recognize" the player after not seeing them for a period of time and they have changed their player skin/vehicle when they had never provided you their name. Also do not type in chat asking or telling eachother where you are on the map, what you are doing, or planning a crime in chat.

 

Fail Role Play or FailRP

FailRP is an event surrounding an action that your character commits that is not probable. Basically doing stuff that are unrealistc.

 

Powergaming is Forbidden

Our definition of powergaming is the abuse of in-game mechanics to give you an unrealistic advantage that benefits the player(s). Also known as using mechanics or roleplaying mechanics that do not exist in the game to your advantage.

 

Stay in Character

Please try and stay in Character at all time unless being spoken to by an Admin or Mod. Staying "In Character" is something quite important when it comes to roleplaying properly. Act like your person would act and make sure to stick to things they would be capable.

 

Cop Baiting

It is forbidden for player's to cop bait officers. If an officer is busy with a crime scene such as a bank robbery or a murder or they are engaged in role play, be sure to let them finish. Don't go honking your horn or doing burnout that will result in a kick.

 

Must Have a Microphone

Players without microphones are subject to being kicked. Lacking a microphone not only ruins the role play experience but causes unnecessary chat spam and ruins the flow of role play. The "N" key is the default push-to-talk while in game.

 

New Life Rules

If your character dies in a realistic manner (i.e. being burned alive, shot multiple times, stabbed, or even involved in a serious vehicle accident), you are not to "remember" anything from your previous life.

 

Pursuit Rules

If your vehicle hits a wall, tree, light pole, or any other object over 40 MPH. Then it is now disabled and you can no longer flee using the vehicle. However, you may get out of your vehicle and flee on foot. If you are able flee and get away, you may not change vehicles or clothing/skin for 5 minutes. You may steal another vehicle on the street if you are able to flee after 5 minutes. At no time in a pursuit you allowed to take your vehicle to a custom shop for repairs.

 

Traffic Laws

Speed limit around the city is 45 MPH. Speed Limit on the highway/freeway are 70 MPH. Parking Lots are 25 MPH. Please obey all traffic laws, if not you will be fined or jail accordingly.

 

Combat Logging

Is prohibited including using respawn while involved in an RP situation is not allowed. Logging out of the server to avoid incarceration, or an RP situation is also prohibited. This will result in a kick.

 

Bank Rules

The bank vault is only open if there are three officers online. Using the bank teller is authorized regardless if officers are on or offline. If players plan on doing a heist, which includes two or more robbers, then there must be three or more officers online. It is the player's responsibility to ask if the bank is open upon logging into the server. Players will not be chastised for asking.

 

Weapons

Legal weapons and a firearm permit are available at the gun store for purchase. There are black market weapons located at Grove Street for purchase, but use those weapon at your own risk.

 

DO NOT SPAM CHAT

The chat box is a privilege and not a right. Abusing it will get you kicked or banned. No matter what the issue is, no player is authorized to spam chat. You will be warned, kicked, then banned. You must use /ooc for any OOC discussions. Please try to keep chat to a minumum.
