USA Realism RP

Getting Started

Character Appearance

You can change your gender and player model at any clothing store marked on your map. You can further customize your character by going to a barber shop. You can also get tattoos by going to a tattoo shop. Make sure you press "save" when finished at the clothing store and "checkout" when finished at the barber shop or you will lose your customizations!

HUD

The two bars under your health (green) and armor (blue) bars indicate your food (yellow) and thirst (purple) levels. Make sure to buy yourself food and water from a general store so you don't pass out from dehydration or hunger! Your money on hand can be seen using /cash or /wallet. You can see how much you have in the bank by typing /bank into chat or by visiting a bank and pressing "E" to open the menu.

Getting a Job

There are various ways to earn money. One way is to try to flex your entrepreneurial skills by starting a business or organization of your own. Another way could be to find a business that is hiring. You can go to any of the icons on the map such as Bubba's Tow Co., Downtown Taxi Co., GoPostal, etc and sign in or begin doing the job to earn money. There are also illegal ways to earn money but they are not marked on the map and you will have ask people (in character) how to access them.

Getting a phone

Go to a "general store" marked on your map and purchase a cell phone. You can then open and use the phone by using the F1 hotkey or doing M > Inventory > Cell Phone > Use.

Inventory Tips

To use, trade, and drop items in your inventory, do M > Inventory and then right click on the item to use or trade.


The first four slots (from the top left) of your inventory correspond to your 1, 2, 3, and 4 keyboard keys. This replaces the default GTA weapon wheel selection method. You can use TAB to store your currently selected weapon. You must have your GTA "weapon select" settings set as default values for this to work as expected.


Weapons

Firearm permits, weapons, and weapon customizations are available at Gun Stores. There are illegal customizations (suppressors, extended magazines, etc) available at unmarked locations around the map. Please be responsible in your use of a firearm. Do not be trigger happy. Limit your use of it.


Most weapons will be set to "safety" mode by default. You can change fire modes by aiming the weapon (hold right click) and pressing C.

Cinematic Mode

You can press F5 to enter cinematic mode. This is great for immersive RP moments!

Common Commands and/or Hotkeys

You can find more commands and hotkeys by clicking the "Command List" button below.

Where is the RP?

We role play using the entire map A.K.A. state-wide RP.

How do I talk?

Make sure your in-game voice settings are configured properly!

How can I get more help?

If you need immediate assistance with something, you can use the /help command. For anything less pressing, you can visit our Discord #support channel or use the website forum. You can type /info at any time to bring up this info sheet.

Rules

RDM & VDM

Before a player can kill another player including the police & EMS, all players must be engaged in the active roleplaying scenario. You are not allowed to kill an individual without any roleplay, this also includes the use of vehicles.

Realistic Behavior

All players shall conduct themselves in a realistic manner. Despite game mechanics, it is the player's responsibility to act realistic. This means don't continue driving like nothing happened after flying off a 200 foot cliff in your vehicle or smashing head on into a vehicle at 65 MPH. Make sure to use your best judgement when it comes to sticking to your role. Understand the boundaries that your character may have and try to stick within those boundaries!

Metagaming

You are not allowed to use out of character information (like server ID numbers, the player list, etc) for in character things. For example, you cannot identify someone just by the player list if you have no valid RP reason to believe it could be that person. Another example would be watching a Twitch stream of another player in the server while playing.

Cop Baiting

It is forbidden for players to deliberately get the attention or commit a crime infront of police officers. If an officer is busy with a crime scene or they are engaged in role play, let them finish. Do not Intentionally hit, ram, shoot, or otherwise act inappropriately toward police personnel without engaging in proper role play.

Combat Logging

Do not intentionally respawn or disconnect from the server while involved in an active roleplaying situation.

New Life Rule

No player shall decide if another is dead. It will be the responsibility of the downed player to decide their own fate. Should a player be able to receive medical help and be revived then the player shall live from the encounter and remember everything about the encounter. Should a player NOT be able to receive medical help but does choose to respawn then they shall not remember the event that leads to them respawning. Should a player decide that they are dead then they shall be sent to the morgue and they are to DELETE THE CHARACTER this means if you are dead then you are ending the character's existence.
