Getting Started

Character Appearance

You can change your gender and player model at any clothing store marked on your map. You can further customize your character after saving a character at the clothing store by going to a barber shop. You can also get tattoos by going to a tattoo shop.

Getting a Job

There are various ways to earn money. One way is to try to flex your entrepreneurial skills by starting a business or organization of your own. Another way could be to find a business that is hiring. You can go to any of the icons on the map such as Downtox Taxi Co., GoPostal, Fridgeit Trucking, Fishing, etc and sign in or begin doing the job to earn money. There are also illegal ways to earn money but they are not marked on the map and you will have ask people (in character) how to access them. Make sure you buy yourself food and water so you don't pass out from dehydration or hunger! The two bars (yellow & purple) under your health and armor bars under the minimap indicate your food and health levels.

How do I get a phone?

Go to a "general store" marked on your map as a green icon and purchase a cell phone. You can then open and use the phone by doing M > Inventory > Cell Phone > Use.

Weapons

Legal weapons and a firearm permit are available at the gun store for purchase. Please be responsible in your use of a firearm. Do not be trigger happy or and limit your use of it. There are black market weapons available for purchase, but use those weapon at your own risk. There are also legal weapon attachments and tints as well as illegal attachments such as suppressors and extended magazines.

Common Commands and/or Hotkeys

Type /commands into chat for a small list of commands. You can use Page Up and Page Down to move up or down in the chat box.

How come I can't hear anyone?

If this is your first time playing FiveM you might have an issue where your voice is not activated. You should make sure your VOIP is activated by pressing ESC and going into your voice settings and making sure it is toggled on. The default VOIP key is N.

How can I get more help?

If you still need further assistance with something, you can use the /help command to speak with a staff member. Please limit your use of this command as much as possible.

Rules

RDM & VDM

Before a player can kill another player including the police & EMS, all players must be engaged in the active roleplaying scenario. You are not allowed to kill an individual without any roleplay, this also includes the use of vehicles.

Realistic Behavior

All players shall conduct themselves in a realistic manner. Despite game mechanics, it is the player's responsibility to act "realistic." Make sure to use your best judgement when it comes to sticking to your role. Understand the boundaries that your character may have and try to stick within those boundaries!

Metagaming

You are not allowed to use out of character information (like server ID numbers, the player list, etc) for in character things. For example, you cannot identify someone just by the player list if you have no valid RP reason to believe it could be that person. Another example would be watching a Twitch stream of another player in the server while playing.

Cop Baiting

It is forbidden for players to deliberately get the attention or commit a crime infront of police officers. If an officer is busy with a crime scene or they are engaged in role play, let them finish. Do not Intentionally hit, ram, shoot, or otherwise act inappropriately toward police personnel without engaging in proper role play.

Combat Logging

Do not intentionally respawn or disconnect from the server while involved in an active roleplaying situation.
